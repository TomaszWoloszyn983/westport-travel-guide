Westport Co.Mayo

Irish: Cathair na Mart, meaning 'city of the beef cattle', historically anglicised as Cahernamart is a town in County Mayo in Ireland. It is at the south-east corner of Clew Bay, an inlet of the Atlantic Ocean on the west coast of Ireland.
